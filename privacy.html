Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 April 2025

This website is a personal hobby project. We are committed to your privacy and keep things simple — we do not collect, store, or share any personal information from visitors.

Information We Do Not Collect

When you visit Card Crafting Love, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location or IP address for tracking purposes
  • Any browsing behaviour or usage data
  • Any financial or payment information

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. No tracking cookies are placed on your device. We do not use any third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics.

User Accounts

This website does not require registration or user accounts of any kind. There is no login, no sign-up, and no membership system. You can browse freely without providing any information.

Card Images and Content

Card images and crafting inspiration are displayed for personal, non-commercial inspiration only. All content is curated by the site owner for the enjoyment of the card crafting community.

Pinterest Content

Where Pinterest content appears on this website, it is accessed via the official Pinterest API for publicly available content only. We access only public pins and boards. We do not access, store, or share any private Pinterest account data. Pinterest's own privacy policy applies to content hosted on their platform.

YouTube Links

Tutorial links to YouTube are provided via the official YouTube Data API v3. Clicking a YouTube link will take you to YouTube's website, where YouTube's own privacy policy applies. We do not embed tracking code alongside YouTube content.

Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub's privacy policy applies to the hosting infrastructure. We do not use any advertising networks, affiliate programmes, or data brokers. No data collected from this website is sold or shared with any third party.

Children's Privacy

This website is a general hobby interest site. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. As we collect no personal information from any visitor, there is no special risk to children using this site.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. As this is a simple hobby website with no data collection, significant changes are unlikely.

Questions?
If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how the site works, this is a personal hobby project and we welcome friendly enquiries. As we collect no personal data, there is very little to ask about — but we are happy to chat about card crafting any time!
