About me

I am a grandmother, a gardener, and a lifelong maker of small paper things. My kitchen table is almost always covered in paper scraps, stamps, ribbons and a very patient cup of tea.

I started making cards because I wanted the people I love to know they had been thought about — not just remembered. A shop card is lovely, but a handmade one carries something else.

This is my little corner where my family and friends can see what I've been up to. Welcome in. ✿
